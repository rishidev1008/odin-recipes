Vegan Tahini Dressing

Delicious Vegan Tahini dressing. Requires only a few ingredients: tahini, lemon, mustard, a sweetener like maple syrup, olive oil, and ice-cold water plus salt and pepper to taste. You can make it ahead of time and keep in fridge. Best if made with a blender, it will take a lot of whisking to blend the tahini without!

Ingredients

  • 1 c tahini
  • 1/4 c olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Combine tahini and oil and blend on high until very smooth.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and blend, adding ice-cold water until desired consistency is reached
