Vegan Marinara Sauce

This is a traditional (and vegan!) red sauce from Italy. The flavor and quality of the canned tomatoes is very important, choose only the very tastiest San Marazano tomoatoes from Italy. Take time cooking, let the flavors blend.

Ingredients

  • 1 large can of San Marazano tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 10 cloves of garlic sliced paper thin
  • sprig of basil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. heat oil on medium in large skillet
  2. when oil is hot add the garlic
  3. in a separate bowl, crush the tomatoes with hands. fill the can halway with water and swirl
  4. when garlic is fragrant and not brown, add the tomatoes andwater.
  5. once bubbling, add the basil sprig. Let it sink by itself into the sauce. Add salt and pepper
  6. When the sauce is thickened and there is an orange oily sheen on top of the sauce, it is done.
